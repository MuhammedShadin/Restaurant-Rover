Our Story

Founded in 2010 by Chef Robert Savory, our restaurant celebrates the art of fine dining with locally-sourced ingredients and innovative flavors.

Each dish tells a story of seasonality and craftsmanship, with our menu changing weekly to showcase the best products from our network of farmers and purveyors.

Our warm, inviting atmosphere provides the perfect setting for memorable dining experiences, from intimate dinners to special celebrations.
